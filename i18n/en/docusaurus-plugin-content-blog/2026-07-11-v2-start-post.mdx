# Torai V2 Is Now Available

## What is Torai V2?

Torai V2 is a redesigned service created in response to the X API's move to pay-per-use pricing.

### Monthly subscriptions

The biggest change is the introduction of monthly subscriptions, allowing us to provide Torai at a lower cost.

### Referral rewards

We also added a referral rewards program.

When someone subscribes using your referral code, you can earn benefits such as free months of Torai.

We would be delighted if you introduced Torai through your website or social channels.

### Significantly stronger security

A personal verification code securely links Torai to your Google Sheet. Nobody except the person signed in to your Torai account can read from or write to that sheet through Torai.

### The same responsive support

We continue to reply within 24 hours, 365 days a year. Please contact us at any time with questions or problems.
