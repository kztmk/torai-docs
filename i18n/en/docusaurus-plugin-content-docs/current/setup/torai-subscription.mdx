# About the Subscription

Torai is a subscription service so that we can continue providing it reliably.

Membership fees may increase as hardware and maintenance costs rise. In principle, however, we aim to let existing members renew at the monthly or annual rate that applied when they joined.

:::caution
Even if a campaign discount applies to your first payment, renewals use the regular monthly or annual price shown when you joined. The discounted price does not apply to renewals.
:::

## Subscription prices

| Term | Price |
| --- | --- |

## How to subscribe

Open [Torai](https://torai.try-try.com).

![Torai home page](/img/setup/g-sheet/step10_torai_signin.jpg)

Sign in with your Google Account.

Accept the terms.

![Accept the terms](/img/setup/g-sheet/step10_agree.jpg)

After accepting, the Subscription page opens.

![Select a subscription](/img/getting-start/torai/torai-subscription-select.jpg)

### Credit-card subscription

For credit cards, you can choose:

- Monthly payments
- Payments every six months
- Annual payments

You can cancel any plan at any time. There is no refund, but you can continue using Torai for the original subscription term. The next renewal will not be processed.

Click **Apply by credit card** to open the payment page.

![Select a subscription](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription01.jpg)

Payments are processed through Stripe, so we never receive your credit-card details.

![Stripe checkout](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription02.jpg)

After payment, the subscription completion page appears. Click the button to open your Stripe customer page, where you can also cancel.

![Subscription complete](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription03.jpg)

## How to cancel

You can cancel Torai at any time and continue using it until the end of the paid term.

Unless you delete your account under **Profile > Account Information**, you can restart at any time.

### Credit card

Open **Profile > Subscription**, then click **Manage or review subscription**.

![Subscription information](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription03.jpg)

Your personal Stripe page opens. Click **Cancel subscription**.

![Cancel subscription](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription04.jpg)

Stripe asks for a reason. Click **Continue to cancellation**.

![Cancellation reason](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription05.jpg)

Click **Cancel subscription**.

![Confirm cancellation](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription06.jpg)

Confirm that the plan is cancelled. You will not be charged at the next renewal.

![Cancellation complete](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription07.jpg)
