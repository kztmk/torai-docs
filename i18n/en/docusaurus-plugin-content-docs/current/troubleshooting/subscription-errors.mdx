# Subscription Is Not Active

If your Torai subscription does not become active, check your subscription and payment status.

## Checklist

- Payment has been completed
- You are signed in with the account used for the subscription
- The subscription renewal date has not passed
- The subscription has not been cancelled and payment has not failed
- Reloading the browser does not change the status

## Before contacting support

Make a note of the account used for the subscription, the date and time the problem occurred, and the error message displayed.
