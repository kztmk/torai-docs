# Referral Rewards Program

Torai has a referral program that rewards you based on the activity of people you refer.

## Find your referral link

1. Sign in to Torai.
2. Open **Profile** from the left menu.
3. Select the **Referral Program** tab.
4. Click **Copy** next to **Referral link**.
5. Share the copied link with the person you want to invite to Torai.

![Referral Program page under Profile](/img/subscription/referral-program.png)

:::caution
To ensure the referral is recorded correctly, ask the person you invite to register through your personal referral link.
:::

## When a reward is confirmed

A reward is confirmed when the person you referred completes their first credit-card payment.

Simply opening the referral link is not enough. The reward is not confirmed until the first payment has been completed after registration.

## Rewards by number of referrals

The Referral Program page shows the following targets and rewards.

| Referrals | Reward |
| ---: | --- |
| 1 | One month free |
| 5 | Three additional months free |
| 10 | Six additional months free |
| 30 | One year free |
| 50 | 50% off for life |
| 100 | Free for life |

**Next reward** shows how many more referrals you need to reach the next target and your current progress.

:::info
Check the Referral Program page for the latest referral count and reward status.
:::

## Check referral and reward status

The page shows the following information:

- **Referral registrations**: People who registered through your referral link
- **Terms accepted**: People who completed acceptance of the Terms of Use
- **Subscriptions started**: People who started a subscription
- **Months earned**: Free subscription time earned through referrals
- **Rewards earned**: Confirmed reward amount
- **Applied**: Reward amount already applied
- **Not applied**: Reward amount not yet applied
- **Estimated redeemed**: Estimated amount of rewards already used

**Available rewards** shows the available Stripe customer balance.

If the information is not current, click **Refresh** in the upper-right corner.
