# Manage Your Subscription

Torai is provided as a subscription service.

You can pay for your subscription by credit card and choose a one-month, six-month, or annual plan.

You can cancel at any time after your subscription starts. You can continue using Torai until the end of the paid term, but payments are non-refundable.

## Check your plan

If you do not have a subscription, Torai automatically takes you to the Subscription page.

If you have a subscription, open **Profile > Subscription** to review it.

You can check:

- Your payment method
- Your next renewal date

### Credit card

Credit-card subscriptions are processed by Stripe.

Your credit-card information is exchanged directly with Stripe. We receive only the details of your subscription.

To cancel, click the subscription management button on the Subscription page. This opens Stripe's page.

![Credit-card subscription](/img/torai/post/subsc1.jpg)

## How to cancel

You can cancel at any time. You will not be charged on the next renewal date.

You can continue using Torai until that date. Payments are non-refundable.

### Credit card

Open **Profile > Subscription**, then click **Manage or review subscription**.

![Subscription information](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription03.jpg)

Your personal Stripe page opens. Click **Cancel subscription**.

![Cancel subscription](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription04.jpg)

Stripe asks for a reason. Click **Continue to cancellation**.

![Cancellation reason](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription05.jpg)

Click **Cancel subscription**.

![Confirm cancellation](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription06.jpg)

Confirm that the plan has been cancelled. You will not be charged at the next renewal.

![Cancellation complete](/img/getting-start/torai/subscription07.jpg)

## Using Torai after your subscription ends

You can restart at any time unless you delete your account from **Profile > Account Information**.

## Receipts and billing history

Review your receipts and billing history on your Stripe customer page.

## Important note

Pay-per-use X API charges are billed separately by X and are not included in your Torai subscription.
