## What is Torai?

Torai is a web application that connects Apps Script, Google Sheets, and Google Drive in your own Google Account to help you create and automatically publish posts to X.

You do not need to make detailed edits to spreadsheet rows and columns. You can create posts as naturally as you would on X, then set their publication times and automated posting options.

![Services used by Torai](/img/intro-google-x-flow.svg)

The following information is stored securely in your own Google Sheet and Google Drive:

- X account information
- Post content and publication times
- Images

## What you can do with Torai

Torai lets you perform the work required to publish to X from one interface.

- Get help creating posts
- Publish at a specified date and time
- Configure automated posting
- Manage post data through Google Drive and Google Sheets
- Receive automated posting status on your phone through Discord notifications

The controls are similar to creating a normal post, so you can get started even if you are not comfortable editing spreadsheets directly.

## Key features

Torai manages X posts using Google's services.

- **<u>⭕ Use Google services as the back end</u>**
  - Store post data in Google Sheets
  - Manage images and videos in Google Drive
  - Run automated posting with Google Apps Script even when your computer is off
- **<u>⭕️ Create posts in a graphical interface</u>**
  - Enter content in the app instead of editing spreadsheet cells

  - Schedule a thread with just a few clicks

  - Make use of spare moments by working from your phone
- **<u>⭕ Manage multiple accounts</u>**

## How Torai differs from other tools

With many automated posting tools, both the data and the posting process are kept inside the service.

Torai stores post content and images in your Google Sheet and Google Drive. Your Apps Script performs the posting, which means you manage both the data location and posting flow in your own Google environment.

You can also create posts, choose images, and set publication times in Torai without making detailed edits directly in the spreadsheet.

## Limits

Torai connects Google Sheets, Google Drive, Google Apps Script, and X, so the limits of each service apply.

### Google limits

Google limits Apps Script execution time, external requests, Google Drive storage, file sizes, and other resources. If you publish in large volumes or use large images or videos, also check your Google usage.

- Total program runtime for communication with Torai and publishing to X: 90 minutes per day
- Runtime for one execution: up to 6 minutes
- External requests: 20,000 per day
- Data sent or received: 50 MB per request
- Triggers: 20

### X limits

On February 6, 2026, X ended its $200/month and $5,000/month services and moved to pay-per-use pricing. Charges are paid using credits purchased in advance or automatic top-ups. You can also set a monthly spending cap.

You can estimate your usage charges on the [X pricing page](https://developer.x.com/#pricing).

## An affordable monthly service

Torai is available as a monthly subscription.

You can start and stop at any time. Unless you delete your account, you can restart whenever you need it again.

:::info
When you stop your subscription, Torai's paid features become unavailable. Restarting the subscription restores access on the same account.
:::

## What you need to get started

Prepare these two accounts before starting:

- A Google Account
- An X account

The Google Account is used for Apps Script, Google Sheets, and Google Drive. The X account is the account that will publish the posts.

## Helpful but optional

A Discord account lets you receive automated posting results and notifications on your phone.

Discord is free and is not required, but it is useful if you want to check posting status while away from your computer.
